Arts and Museums

Through history to art

Can you imagine being so famous as to appear on a bill from your country? Well, the famous painter Juan Manuel Blanes did it. Juan Manuel Blanes is one of the best painters in the world, especially from Uruguay. He is better known as the painter of the country. Thanks to his popularity, there is a museum in Montevideo named after him. In this museum are almost all the paintings that he has done. His most important painting is called "open council" which he made in 1870. The best museums in all of Uruguay are: Museo Ralli, Museum of mar, Andes Museum, Casapueblo Workshop Museum, Taranco Palace, Soccer Museum. These museums are quite important, since in each of them there is a part of the culture of this wonderful country.
