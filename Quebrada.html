Quebrada de los Cuervos

The Place to Discover New Things

"Do not judge books by their cover", this phrase could be referred to this fantastic place, Quebrada de los Cuervos. This place is in the city "thirty-three" in Uruguay. It is a place with a lot of nature, which has magical waterfalls, many different varieties of birds and plants. There are approximately 140 species of birds and 130 of plants. Quebrada de los Cuervos is a national park that has been protected by many signs and paths so that people can pass through and see the beautiful views. There are some beautiful waterfalls where you can take a dip and take beautiful photos to have many memorable memories if you go with your family or friends. The walk through the entire mountain is quite long, it is recommended to bring water and sunscreen so as not to burn your face from the sun, by the way, you exercise as you walk. Something curious about this place is that in the past this area was unknown, but on August 21, 1986, Major Wilson Elso Goñi declared that Quebrada de los Cuervos would now be a protected natural area. species of animals that were there, so this place became a tourist spot.
