Sierra de las Ánimas

The Souls Place

From a volcanic place, to a wonderful mountain range. Sierra de las animas is a little mountain in Uruguay, where you can enjoy the beautiful views of the entire southern part of the country. In the mountain, there is a fantastic tree tunnel that makes the views more enjoyable and gives some shade for you to take pictures or to protect you from the sun. There are some waterfalls and gorges that can be found in the mountain, if you go you should bring some bathing suits, because it's a really hot place and you will like to take a bath. Sierra de las animas was the remains of a volcanic place. With the past of the time it transformed to the fantastic mountain we all know. According to the legend, if you go late in the night, you might meet the souls that live there, these ghosts are the souls of the indigenas charrúas. Charrúas were an indigenous nomadic people who lived in the south of uruguay. This place was really important when the Spanish colonized the region for the charrúas, because this place was where the majority of the Charrúas lived years ago. The name of the mountain was given because of the souls that appear in the night.
