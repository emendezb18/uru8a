Montevideo

The City of Wonders

Pack your bags and recover the wonders of Montevideo. This city is the capital of Uruguay and is one of the most important capitals in southern Latin America. There is a place called Colonia del Sacramento, it is a very important place in Uruguay because of its history. In Montevideo is the Plaza Independencia, which was formerly a Spanish citadel. You can also go to visit the Legislative Palace, this building houses the Legislative Power of Uruguay. The building was inaugurated on August 25, 1925 in honor of the centenary of the declaration of independence. This city is one of the most modern in Uruguay, in addition to this its beaches are the best, they have beautiful sand and the colors of the waters are spectacular. The most recommended place to go is "playa pocitos", it is in Montevideo and it is a pretty nice beach, apart from sunbathing, on summer nights there are many events, which make this beach more lively and beautiful.
