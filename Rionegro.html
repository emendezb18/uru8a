Rio Negro

A Great River for Adventure

The Rio Negro is one of the most important rivers in Uruguay. One reason for this is that it provides water and food to 45 registed indigenous communities in the country, also some goups of descendants from African slaves communities. It is a very touristic place for adventurous people so it helps the economy of people that work on touristic things there and also for the country. People also like to go and see the different types of lifestyles and learn new things with different experiences that a new space can bring you.
